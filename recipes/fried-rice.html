Fried Rice

Did you know that Fried Rice is actually the most nutritious of the recipes here? (Probably.)
Go here for some McNuggets, and here for some French fries.

Description

It's delicious, it's nutritious, and if you want, you can add enough spice to send yourself to orbit. It's the humble Fried Rice!

Ingredients

  • Dried mushrooms
  • Peanut oil
  • Steak sirloin
  • Beaten eggs
  • Left over fried rice
  • Your "secret sauce" (if you want).

Steps

  1. If you are making sauce to go along with it, make the sauce. If not, ignore this.
  2. Cook your vegetables and meat together over high heat. Don't forget the peanut oil unless you want to do a Superman impression prying the food off the pan.
  3. Cook the meat until it's well done, then transfer it to a bowl.
  4. Add enough oil to cook the veggies until they're crisp - drop them in the same bowl as the meat.
  5. Scramble the eggs.
  6. Cook the rice until it's golden-brown. Mmm, tasty...
  7. Dump everything in the skillet, cook it some more, and serve it hot and fresh. Bon appétit!
